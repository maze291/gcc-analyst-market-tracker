# Provider Permission Questions

Send these questions before treating an API as a production source.

## Requested Use

We are evaluating a non-commercial, public labor-market intelligence dashboard for aggregate analyst-job trends in UAE and Saudi Arabia. The dashboard would not publish full postings, descriptions, recruiter data, or direct application links.

## Questions

1. May we publish aggregate public statistics derived from your API results, such as monthly vacancy counts by country/city and analyst role category?
2. May we store normalized metadata and derived fields such as normalized title, city, country, posting date/age, salary range where listed, employment type, work arrangement, and extracted skill labels?
3. May we temporarily cache raw responses locally for quality review and debugging, then discard full descriptions and full application URLs?
4. May we extract skill counts from descriptions/snippets without retaining the source text?
5. Are there attribution requirements for aggregate statistics?
6. Are there limits on refresh frequency, sample size, or retention duration?
7. Are there restricted downstream uses for public dashboards, research reports, or non-commercial analytics?
8. Do your terms permit use of source domains in broad aggregate source-mix reporting?

## Required Written Comfort

Production use remains blocked unless the provider clearly permits aggregate public statistics, derived-field storage, short-term caching, and non-commercial public reporting.
