# Public Display Policy

The public MVP must show aggregate labor-market intelligence only.

## Allowed

- total analyst postings by country
- total analyst postings by city
- weekly or monthly posting trend
- analyst role mix
- aggregate skill counts
- salary coverage rate
- salary ranges only where enough postings contain salary data
- employment type distribution
- remote/hybrid/onsite distribution when field quality is good
- broad source mix categories

## Blocked

- full job postings
- full job descriptions
- recruiter names
- recruiter emails
- phone numbers
- hiring manager names
- LinkedIn profile links
- direct application links
- restricted-platform links
- searchable job-posting database
- raw API exports
