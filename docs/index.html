Method

Directional career intelligence, not a job board.

What it measures

Sampled online job-market data is reviewed, deduplicated where possible, categorized, and converted into aggregate market signals.

What it avoids

The public layer does not display full job listings, direct application links, recruiter contact details, emails, phone numbers, or full job descriptions.

How to read it

Counts represent reviewed roles in this dataset, not a complete count of every open job in the Gulf. Signals are strongest when patterns repeat across snapshots.
