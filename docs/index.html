Method

Aggregate labor-market analytics, not a job board

What the project measures

The tracker summarizes demand by country, city, analyst role category, skill group, salary coverage, employment type, and work arrangement where the underlying fields are reliable.

What it does not publish

The site does not display full job postings, full descriptions, recruiter names, recruiter emails, phone numbers, hiring manager names, profile links, or direct application links.

How source data is handled

Raw samples are used temporarily for review and normalization. Public output is limited to aggregate charts and summary statistics.

Current scope

Feasibility first, public launch only after source permission is clear

Markets

United Arab Emirates and Saudi Arabia.

Role categories

Data Analyst, Business Analyst, BI Analyst, Product Analyst, Financial Analyst, Operations Analyst, Reporting Analyst, and analyst-adjacent Data Scientist.

Project type

Personal research prototype intended for aggregate market insight, not recruitment, direct job-board scraping, or republication of job listings.
