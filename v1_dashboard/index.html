What the numbers mean

This dashboard summarizes reviewed feasibility samples from JSearch and Careerjet. Counts show what passed review in the sample, not every analyst job in the region.

What is safe to publish

Only aggregate statistics are shown. Full postings, full descriptions, full application URLs, recruiter names, emails, phone numbers, and profile links are excluded.

What is still blocked

Public launch stays blocked until providers confirm that aggregate public reporting and derived-field storage are allowed.

How to read weak signals

Salary and trend charts need more recurring samples before they can support confident conclusions.
