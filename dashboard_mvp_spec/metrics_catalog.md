# Metrics Catalog

## Core Metrics

- total analyst postings by country
- total analyst postings by city
- weekly or monthly posting trend
- analyst role mix
- top skills by aggregate count
- salary coverage rate
- salary range distribution where enough postings list salary
- employment type distribution
- work arrangement distribution where field quality is good
- restricted-source share
- duplicate rate

## Analyst Role Categories

- Data Analyst
- Business Analyst
- BI Analyst
- Product Analyst
- Financial Analyst
- Operations Analyst
- Reporting Analyst
- Analyst-adjacent Data Scientist

## Quality Metrics

- title relevance rate
- company presence rate
- country presence rate
- city presence rate
- posting date or age presence rate
- description/snippet usefulness rate
- PII/contact-data risk rate
